## 🔬 Professional Summary

I am a **Teaching Assistant** and **Postgraduate Researcher** at **Luxor University** and **Sohag University**, specializing in **Blockchain Technologies** and **Post-Quantum Cryptography (PQC)**.

My research bridges **cryptographic engineering**, **distributed systems**, and **explainable AI** to build next-generation secure infrastructure. I am particularly focused on:

- **Quantum-Resistant Blockchain Protocols:** Designing and analyzing consensus mechanisms and signature schemes that remain secure against both classical and quantum adversaries.
- **Post-Quantum Cryptography (PQC) Integration:** Engineering crypto-agile middleware, hybrid signature frameworks, and migration strategies for healthcare and enterprise systems.
- **Explainable AI in Cybersecurity:** Developing interpretable ML pipelines for memory forensics, fileless malware detection, and intelligent threat hunting.
- **Secure Distributed Systems:** Building tamper-proof authentication layers, decentralized identity solutions, and secure data provenance mechanisms.
